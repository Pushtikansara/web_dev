Welcome to CHARUSAT Student Portal!

CHARUSAT (Charotar University of Science and Technology), located in Changa, Gujarat, is a reputed private university established in 2009. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, pharmacy, management, computer science, and more. Known for fostering innovation, research, and collaboration with industries, CHARUSAT provides a dynamic environment for academic and professional growth. The university is committed to holistic development and real-world learning to empower future leaders and change-makers.
