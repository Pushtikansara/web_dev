Welcome to CHARUSAT Student Portal!

CHARUSAT (Charotar University of Science and Technology) is a private university located in Changa, Gujarat, India. Established in 2009, it offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, pharmacy, and other fields. The university is known for its focus on research, innovation, and industry collaboration, providing students with opportunities to engage in practical learning experiences. CHARUSAT aims to foster a holistic educational environment that prepares students for successful careers in their respective fields.
