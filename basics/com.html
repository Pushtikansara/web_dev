Computer Engineering

Computer engineering is a field that combines principles of electrical engineering and computer science to develop computer systems and hardware. It involves designing, building, and maintaining computer systems, including both hardware components like processors and memory, and software components like operating systems and applications. Computer engineers work on a wide range of technologies, from embedded systems in everyday devices to large-scale computing systems used in data centers. Their work is crucial for advancements in technology, enabling innovations in areas such as artificial intelligence, robotics, and the Internet of Things (IoT).
