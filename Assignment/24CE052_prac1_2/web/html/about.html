About CHARUSAT

CHARUSAT (Charotar University of Science and Technology), located in Changa, Gujarat, is one of India's top-rated private universities. Established in 2009 and recognized by UGC, CHARUSAT offers a wide range of programs in engineering, pharmacy, management, sciences, and healthcare. With a strong focus on research, innovation, and quality education, the university empowers students with real-world skills and values. Its mission is to develop globally competitive professionals equipped with knowledge, ethics, and a passion for excellence.
